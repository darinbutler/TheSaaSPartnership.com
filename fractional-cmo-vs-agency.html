Fractional CMO vs Agency

Fractional CMO or SaaS marketing agency? Here's the honest answer.

For most growth-stage SaaS companies, neither a traditional agency nor a solo fractional CMO is the right answer. What you need is senior strategic leadership combined with a specialist execution team — without paying for both separately.

Find the Right Model See the comparison
The Trade-Offs

What you gain and lose with each model

📋 Traditional Agency

Execution without strategic ownership

Agencies deliver activities — campaigns, content, reports. What they rarely provide is strategic CMO-level ownership: board engagement, commercial model alignment, team building or budget accountability. You get outputs, not leadership.

👤 Solo Fractional CMO

Strategy without execution capacity

A solo fractional CMO provides senior strategic thinking but has limited execution bandwidth. You still need to source, brief and manage specialist teams — adding management overhead at precisely the moment you need things to move fast.

✓ The TSP Model

Strategy and execution in one team

TSP provides senior strategic leadership embedded with a specialist execution team. The CMO-level strategist and the paid, SEO, CRO and automation specialists are the same team — aligned, accountable and moving together.
