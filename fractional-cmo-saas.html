Definition

What is a fractional CMO?

A fractional CMO is a senior marketing executive who works with your SaaS company on a part-time or project basis, providing the same strategic leadership, channel expertise and revenue accountability as a full-time CMO — without the salary, equity, benefits and ramp time.

Unlike traditional marketing agencies that execute campaigns, a fractional CMO sits in your corner office (virtually). They own your marketing metrics, manage your team, and hold themselves accountable to pipeline and ARR impact.

You get executive-level thinking, real-world SaaS experience, and complete flexibility. No long-term employment contracts. No equity negotiations. No 6-month onboarding.

Fit

When you need a fractional CMO

Sound familiar? Here are the most common reasons SaaS companies bring on a fractional CMO.

You have a product that sells but no scalable pipeline engine

You're still dependent on founder-led sales or bottom-up product adoption. You need someone to build real go-to-market.

Your marketing team lacks senior leadership

Individual contributors are doing good work but without strategy or coordination. You need someone to set direction and hold the line on metrics.

You've just raised Series A

You've got capital and product-market fit. Now you need senior marketing expertise to build the function, not just execute campaigns.

Your CAC is too high

You don't know which channel is the problem or how to optimise across channels. You need someone with the data fluency to diagnose and fix.

You've absorbed a team through M&A

You now have multiple marketing teams pulling in different directions. You need someone to own the integration and consolidate around unified metrics.

You're 18 months from exit

Your marketing metrics need to stack up for acquisition or IPO. You need someone to build the measurement and attribution infrastructure that VCs respect.

Common Questions

Fractional CMO FAQs

How many hours does a fractional CMO work per month? ▾
It depends on the plan. Our Startup plan is 1–2 days/week (20–40 hours). Growth plan is 2–3 days/week (40–60 hours). Scale plan is near full-time (60–80+ hours). All hours are billable, and you're paying for senior expertise, not junior time.
Can a fractional CMO manage our existing marketing team? ▾
Absolutely. In fact, team management is built into our Growth and Scale plans. Your fractional CMO will provide day-to-day leadership, performance feedback, and strategic direction. You keep your team; they just get real executive oversight and career development from someone senior.
What's the minimum engagement length? ▾
We recommend a minimum of 6 months to establish real impact and trust. Most partnerships run 12–24 months. If you want to try before committing, we offer Growth Sprints — intensive 4-week programs designed to prove value and alignment.
What's the difference between a fractional CMO and a marketing consultant? ▾
A consultant advises; a fractional CMO executes and owns results. You're not paying for deck-ware or recommendations. You're buying revenue accountability. They sit in on sales calls, manage campaigns personally, build your measurement system, and are answerable to your board.
How quickly will we see results? ▾
First month: clarity and diagnostics. By Month 2–3: first campaigns live and initial results. By Month 4–6: meaningful pipeline impact and CAC improvements. Real marketing transformation doesn't happen overnight, but a senior CMO moves much faster than hiring your own.
