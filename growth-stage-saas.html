The Growth Challenges

Common roadblocks to scaling SaaS marketing

👥

No senior marketing leadership

Junior team executing without strategy or ICP clarity. Campaigns run independently rather than toward a shared goal.

📈

CAC climbing faster than ARR

Paid channels getting more expensive, conversion rates flat, and no clear path to unit economics that work.

🔍

SEO producing traffic but not pipeline

Wrong keywords, no bottom-of-funnel content, and traffic that doesn't move the revenue dial.

🤝

Marketing and sales misaligned

Inconsistent MQL definitions, no shared attribution, and finger-pointing when targets miss.
