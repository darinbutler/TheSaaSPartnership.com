Who We Are

We started The SaaS Partnership because the agency model was broken

A collective of senior SaaS marketing specialists who left the agency world to build something better — a model that puts expertise, accountability and results at the centre of everything.

Our Story

Built by practitioners who were frustrated with the status quo

The founding team of The SaaS Partnership all share the same story. Years working in agencies and in-house SaaS marketing teams — watching talented people deliver mediocre results because the model they were working in didn't allow for genuine specialist depth or real performance accountability.

Agencies charged large retainers while rotating junior account managers through client accounts. In-house teams were hired quickly in growth phases and left with inflated headcount when the market shifted. Neither model was built to deliver what scaling SaaS companies actually need: highly experienced specialists who are genuinely accountable to revenue outcomes.

So we built a different model. One where the incentives are aligned, the expertise is real, and the commercial structure reflects the value delivered — not the hours billed or the headcount maintained.

The SaaS Partnership exists because we believe SaaS companies deserve better than the models they've been sold. Better expertise. Better alignment. Better results.

Our Values

What drives everything we do

🎯

Outcomes over activity

We measure ourselves on pipeline contribution and revenue impact — not hours billed, reports written or meetings attended. If it doesn't move your growth needle, we don't do it.

🤝

Partnership, not vendor

We work as a genuine extension of your team. That means honest conversations, strategic challenge and complete transparency — not just executing briefs and sending invoices.

🧠

Specialists, not generalists

Every channel deserves someone who has lived and breathed it for years. We never stretch a good marketer across disciplines they're not expert in. Depth matters.

🔬

Test, learn, scale

We don't assume — we test. Every strategy is validated against data before we scale investment. This discipline protects your budget and accelerates compounding growth.

💡

Proactive, not reactive

We bring ideas to the table. We identify opportunities you haven't thought of yet. We challenge the brief when we think there's a better way. That's what a real partner does.

📊

Radical transparency

You see everything. The data, the attribution, the test results — good and bad. We believe the best partnerships are built on complete visibility, not curated good news.
